# Department Mapping Rules

This reference provides detailed rules for mapping symptoms to appropriate medical departments.

## Abdominal Pain Quadrant Mapping

### 3×3 Abdominal Grid System

```
       Left    Center    Right
       ────   ─────   ────
Upper  │ 上左    上中     上右
Middle │ 中左    中中     中右
Lower  │ 下左    下中     下右
```

### Quadrant-to-Department Mapping

| Quadrant | Associated Departments | Key Symptoms |
|----------|---------------------|--------------|
| **上左 (Upper Left)** | 消化内科、心内科 | 胃痛、脾脏问题、心脏相关疼痛 |
| **上中 (Upper Center)** | 消化内科 | 胃炎、胃溃疡、上腹部疼痛 |
| **上右 (Upper Right)** | 肝胆外科、消食内科 | 肝脏、胆囊（胆囊炎）、胆结石 |
| **中左 (Middle Left)** | 消食内科、外科 | 结肠、脾曲、左侧肠道问题 |
| **中中 (Middle Center)** | 消食内科、全科医学科 | 小肠、全身腹部不适 |
| **中右 (Middle Right)** | 外科、消食内科 | 升结肠、右侧肠道问题 |
| **下左 (Lower Left)** | 消食内科、泌尿外科 | 乙状结肠、左侧输尿管、卵巢（女性） |
| **下中 (Lower Center)** | 消食内科、泌尿外科、妇科 | 膀胱、子宫、前列腺、下肠道 |
| **下右 (Lower Right)** | 普外科（阑尾优先）、消食内科 | **阑尾**（急性阑尾炎！）、盲肠、右侧卵巢 |

## Emergency Indicators

### 立即去急诊科

如果出现以下任何症状，**立即去急诊科**：

**腹部急症征象：**
- 剧烈腹部疼痛伴**板状腹**（腹肌紧张，腹部变硬如木板）
- 高烧（>39°C）伴剧烈腹痛
- 疼痛放射到肩膀（可能为异位妊娠破裂或脾脏问题）
- 呕血或便血（量较多）
- 突然剧烈疼痛伴晕厥或昏迷

**全身急症征象：**
- 呼吸困难、气短
- 胸痛伴压榨感或挤压感
- 严重头痛伴颈部僵硬或意识不清
- 高烧（>39°C）伴严重症状
- 严重外伤或近期手术并发症
- 持续呕吐导致脱水

### 紧急但非危重（急诊或门诊均可）

- 中度发烧（38-39°C）伴腹痛
- 疼痛持续>24小时无改善
- 剧烈疼痛但无急症征象
- 症状突然出现且令人担忧

## Symptom-to-Department Mapping

### Gastroenterology (消化内科)

**Primary symptoms:**
- Upper abdominal pain (upper center, upper left)
- Nausea, vomiting, diarrhea
- Gastritis, peptic ulcer symptoms
- Dyspepsia, bloating
- Chronic abdominal pain

**Question focus:** Eating history, food intake, timing relative to meals

### General Surgery (普外科/普通外科)

**Primary symptoms:**
- **Lower right** quadrant pain (appendicitis suspicion)
- Upper right quadrant pain (gallbladder, liver)
- Severe abdominal pain with suspected surgical cause
- Trauma to abdomen
- Acute cholecystitis, appendicitis, hernia

**Question focus:** Pain character, onset, progression, associated symptoms

### General Medicine (全科医学科/普通内科)

**Primary symptoms:**
- Vague or generalized abdominal pain
- Mild abdominal discomfort without clear localization
- Multiple mild symptoms
- Fever with general malaise
- Chronic/recurring symptoms without clear pattern

**Question focus:** Overall health, timeline, other body systems

### Gynecology (妇科)

**Female patients with:**
- Lower abdominal pain (lower left, lower center, lower right)
- Pain related to menstrual cycle
- Vaginal bleeding or discharge
- Pregnancy possibility (missed period, etc.)
- Ovarian cyst symptoms

**Question focus:** Last menstrual period, pregnancy possibility, cycle relation

### Urology (泌尿外科)

**Primary symptoms:**
- Lower abdominal pain with urinary symptoms
- Flank pain (kidney)
- Burning urination, frequent urination
- Blood in urine
- Kidney stone symptoms

**Question focus:** Urinary symptoms, color of urine, frequency, urgency

### Pediatrics (儿科)

**Patients <14 years with:**
- Any abdominal pain
- Fever, vomiting, diarrhea
- General pediatric symptoms

**Question focus:** Age, developmental stage, pediatric-specific symptoms

## Abdominal Pain Decision Tree

```
Abdominal Pain
    │
    ├─► Lower Right Quadrant?
    │       └─► Suspect Appendicitis → General Surgery (Urgent)
    │
    ├─► Upper Right Quadrant?
    │       ├─► Fever + Severe Pain → Hepatobiliary Surgery (Urgent)
    │       └─► Mild/Moderate → Gastroenterology
    │
    ├─► Upper Center/Upper Left?
    │       ├─► Fever + Severe → Emergency
    │       └─► Gastroenterology
    │
    ├─► Lower Center with Urinary Symptoms?
    │       └─► Urology
    │
    ├─► Lower Quadrants (Female)?
    │       ├─► Menstrual Cycle Related → Gynecology
    │       ├─► Pregnancy Possibility → Gynecology (Urgent)
    │       └─► Gastroenterology or Surgery
    │
    ├─► High Fever (>39°C) with Severe Pain?
    │       └─► Emergency Department
    │
    └─► General/Mild Symptoms?
        └─► General Medicine
```

## Common Symptom Patterns

### 阑尾炎

**症状：**
- 疼痛始于肚脐周围，转移至**下右腹**
- 活动、咳嗽时疼痛加剧
- 低烧（37.5-38.5°C）
- 恶心、呕吐、食欲不振
- 反跳痛

**推荐科室：** 普外科（紧急）

**推荐原因：** 典型阑尾炎表现为转移性右下腹痛，伴低热和消化道症状，需要普外科评估阑尾切除

**紧急程度：** 门诊或急诊（视严重程度）

---

### 胆囊炎

**症状：**
- **上右腹**疼痛
- 疼痛放射到右肩或背部
- 发热（通常较高）
- 恶心、呕吐
- 进食油腻食物后疼痛加重

**推荐科室：** 肝胆外科或普外科

**推荐原因：** 上右腹疼痛伴发热和进食油腻后加重，符合胆囊炎特征，需肝胆外科评估

**紧急程度：** 门诊或急诊（视严重程度）

---

### 胃炎/胃溃疡

**症状：**
- 上中或上左腹疼痛
- 烧灼感
- 饥饿时疼痛（胃炎）或进食后疼痛（胃溃疡）
- 恶心、腹胀
- 通常无发热

**推荐科室：** 消食内科

**推荐原因：** 上腹部疼痛伴烧灼感，疼痛与进食能量相关，符合胃炎或胃溃疡表现，建议消食内科诊治

**紧急程度：** 门诊

---

### 胃肠炎

**症状：**
- 全腹部疼痛或痉挛
- 腹泻（水样）
- 恶心、呕吐
- 可能有发热（通常低烧）
- 近期有不洁饮食史

**推荐科室：** 消食内科或全科医学科

**推荐原因：** 全腹痛伴腹泻、恶心呕吐，近期有不洁饮食史，符合胃肠炎表现，建议消食内科诊治

**紧急程度：** 门诊（高热或严重脱水除外）

---

### 肾结石

**症状：**
- 腰痛剧烈，放射到腹股沟
- 突然、剧烈发作
- 恶心、呕吐
- 血尿
- 躁躁不安，无法找到舒适体位

**推荐科室：** 泌尿外科

**推荐原因：** 剧痛伴放射到腹股沟、血尿、突然剧烈发作，符合肾结石特征，建议泌尿外科诊治

**紧急程度：** 门诊或急诊（视疼痛程度）

## Fever Timeline Questions

### Critical: Pain vs. Fever Chronology

**"先痛还是先发烧?"** (Did pain come first or fever first?)

- **Fever first, then pain:** Suggests infectious origin
  - Gastroenteritis, appendicitis
  - May indicate infectious disease

- **Pain first, then fever:** Suggests inflammatory/obstructive process
  - Cholecystitis, appendicitis
  - Tissue ischemia or necrosis

- **Fever >38.5°C:** Generally requires urgent care
- **Fever >39°C with severe pain:** Emergency

## Associated Symptom Screening

### Must Ask About (for abdominal complaints):

1. **Gastrointestinal:**
   - 恶心
   - 呕吐
   - 腹泻
   - 便秘

2. **Urinary:**
   - 尿频
   - 尿急
   - 尿痛
   - 尿血

3. **General:**
   - 发烧
   - 食欲
   - 体重

4. **Warning signs:**
   - 便血
   - 呕血
   - 尿血
   - 黑便

## Special Populations

### Pediatric Patients (<14 years)

- Always refer to Pediatrics for initial evaluation
- Pediatric abdominal pain patterns differ from adults
- Higher risk of certain conditions (intussusception, Meckel's diverticulum)

### Elderly Patients (>65 years)

- Atypical presentations common
- May have less severe pain despite serious conditions
- Higher risk of complications
- Low threshold for seeking urgent care

### Pregnant Patients

- Pregnancy-related issues → Obstetrics & Gynecology
- Non-pregnancy issues → Gastroenterology/Surgery with pregnancy consideration
- Ectopic pregnancy is life-threatening emergency

### Female Patients of Reproductive Age

- Always ask about last menstrual period
- Consider gynecological causes for lower abdominal pain
- Evaluate pregnancy possibility
